The function mapping the 'latent ' 2d data to its 3d representation is the embedding , and the underlying 2d space itself is the latent space ( or embedded space ) : Synonyms Depending on the specific impression you wish to give , `` embedding '' often goes by different terms : Term Context dimensionality reduction combating the `` curse of dimensionality '' feature extraction feature projection feature embedding feature learning representation learning extracting 'meaningful ' features from raw data embedding manifold learning latent feature representation understanding the underlying topology of the data However this is not a hard-and-fast rule , and they are often completely interchangeable..
